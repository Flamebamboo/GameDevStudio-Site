About Us

We’re a group of Godot enthusiasts formed in the Godot server, our studio consists of passionate developers, writers, and artists. We are hoping to release a 2D Platformer that follows the concept of Metroidvania...

Cavern Of The Soul Shards

Cavern Of The Soul Shard is a metroidvania platformer following our protagonist Grin as he fights through hordes of enemies to save the princess that he has sworn to protect since childhood, Lilianthe.
She gets kidnapped by The Crypt Lord who wants to use her life force to power his Soul Shard to strengthen himself and extend his lifespan.
While chasing after them, Grin falls into a cavern where he’ll meet a few helpful people, but mostly deadly monsters that want nothing more than to stop Grin in his tracks. He fights his way through various levels with different environments until he reaches The Crypt Lord and Lilianthe.
